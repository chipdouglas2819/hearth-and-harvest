v6 fixes: The boon and plant button issues were from render rebuilding the entire plot DOM every frame at 600× speed — clicks were being lost mid-event. Tick now does light DOM updates (progress bar, time text) without rebuilding. Tap effect scales with speed (≈2% at 1×, ≈11% at 600×) and now animates visibly. Time-mult buffs no longer un-ready a ripe plot when applied late. Plant modal now shows "need ◉ X more" so it's clear why options are locked. Wishflower/Cornucopia no longer waste their charge on closing the modal. Starting balance: ◉ 25 — exactly one radish. Monetization (TBD): the right model — battle-pass-style permanent upgrades, ad-free purchase, or a subscription — depends on the meta-game we haven't built yet. Removed the per-pack price tag. The Watch-Ad-for-Bonus-Card button stays in pack openings as the rewarded video pattern.
